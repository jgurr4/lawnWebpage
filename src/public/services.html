Services

(Prices are for yards 450 Sq. Ft. or smaller)

Mowing/Edging

For this basic service, I will bring my equipment to your residence and cut the grass to a healthy desired height based on the type of grass you have. Then I will take any grass clippings off-site to dispose of them myself, or reintroduce them to your yard/plants for extra nitrogen if you want. This service is available to schedule weekly, biweekly, or monthly.

Weed Control

Part of keeping a healthy yard is also preventing invasive plants from becoming part of it. The weeds can easily multiply when left alone for too long making even a otherwise healthy looking grass lawn cluttered and much less appealing. For this service I will spray the weeds with a herbicide tailored specifically to kill that particular weed species while also avoiding damage to the surrounding grass. There are also methods of prevention that are important to understand in the fight against weeds.

Leaf Blowing/Sweeping

Is your patio cluttered with leaves or debris? Need someone to de-clutter all the stuff that is tarnishing the beauty of your home? Then this is where we can come in and sweep/leaf blow any messes that are currently plaguing your walkways, and we'll take it away with us to dump elsewhere.

Trimming, Pruning, and Shaping shrubs/bushes

Are the bushes getting overgrown to the point that they just look like a mess of branches/leaves? Do you wish that they were a little neater or shaped in a more pleasing way? We can help with that! We'll bring our tools to cut away excess branches and shape the plants to whatever size or shape you best prefer.

Aeration

Aeration is a technique where small holes are created and spaced evenly apart throughout a grass lawn with the intention of loosening the soil to allow more nutrients /water to reach the roots. Lawns with compacted soil can benefit from this since the hard soil causes the grass roots to have a harder time reaching deeper, and also makes any fertilizer/grass seeds/water have a harder time sinking into the soil to be absorbed by grass roots.

Overseeding

Is your lawn thinner than most? Do you see bare spots of dirt between the blades of grass? Or do you just need to have a different type of grass seed to prepare for winter? If so, this is the service you'll need! Just tell us what the need is, and the type of seed you want, and we'll use a custom technique to ensure any thin/bare spots get the best opportunity to fill in.

Fertilizing

Fertilizer is best for lawns which seem to be losing their lush color. The blades of grass may be looking thinner, droopy and dry looking. And they will be more susceptible to dying when being stepped on. For this service, we would bring some high quality fertilizer and spread it around the entire lawn with a spreader tool. Get the best looking yard by opting in for this service.
